Handprint

Environmental handprint refers to positive actions that individuals, businesses, or activities take to enhance the environment. It contrasts with the environmental footprint by measuring beneficial contributions like conservation efforts, sustainable practices, and eco-friendly initiatives.

Footprint

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases, primarily carbon dioxide, produced directly or indirectly by an individual, organization, event, or product, indicating its impact on climate change.

What are SDG's?

Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a set of 17 global objectives established by the United Nations to address pressing challenges, including poverty, inequality, climate change, and environmental degradation, aiming for a more sustainable and equitable world by 2030.
